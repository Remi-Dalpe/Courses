How to play

  1. Rolling the Dice:
    • Click the "Roll Dice" button to roll the dice.
    • The dice will generate a random number between 1 and 6.
  2. Scoring:
    • If the number rolled is 1, your current score is reset to 0, and it becomes the next player's turn.
    • If the number rolled is between 2 and 6, the number is added to your current score.
  3. Holding Your Score:
    • You can choose to Hold at any time by clicking the "Hold" button.
    • Holding will add your current score to your total score and end your turn, passing the dice to the next player.
  4. Winning the Game:
    • The first player to reach a total score of 100 wins the game!
  5. Starting a New Game:
    • Click the "New Game" button to reset all scores and start a new game.
